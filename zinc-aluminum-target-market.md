<p><p>The Zinc Aluminum Target market comprises two main types: plane targets and rotating targets. Plane targets are flat surfaces used for applications requiring a stable, consistent deposition of metal, ideal for coating and thin film technologies. In contrast, rotating targets are designed to enhance material deposition efficiency by allowing the target to spin during the coating process, improving uniformity and prolonging target life. Both types cater to diverse industries, including electronics, automotive, and aerospace, where high-quality coatings are essential.</p></p>
