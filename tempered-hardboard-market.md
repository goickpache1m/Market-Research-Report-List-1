<p><p>The tempered hardboard market primarily consists of three key types: MDF (Medium Density Fiberboard), plywood, and traditional hardboard. MDF is known for its smooth finish and versatility, making it ideal for furniture and cabinetry. Plywood, composed of thin wood veneers, offers strength and aesthetic appeal, often used in construction and interior design. Traditional hardboard, made from wood fibers, provides durability and is commonly used for wall paneling. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to various industry needs.</p></p>
