<p><strong>The Automotive Steering Gearbox Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric Power Steering Gearbox</li><li>Hydraulic Power Steering Gearbox</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive steering gearbox market consists of two primary types: Electric Power Steering (EPS) and Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS) gearboxes. EPS utilizes electric motors to assist steering, offering advantages like enhanced fuel efficiency and reduced weight. In contrast, HPS relies on hydraulic fluid pressure to provide steering assistance, delivering a traditional feel and consistent performance. Both systems aim to improve vehicle handling and driver comfort, but EPS is gaining popularity due to technological advancements and increasing fuel economy regulations.</p></p>
