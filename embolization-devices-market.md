<p><p>The embolization devices market includes various types designed for vascular occlusion and treatment of conditions like aneurysms and tumors. Embolic coils are used for packing and occluding blood vessels. Liquid agents, such as adhesives, create emboli when injected. EPDs (Embolic Protection Devices) prevent debris migration during procedures. Flow diverters redirect blood flow away from targeted vessels to promote healing. Access devices facilitate the delivery of these treatments. Each type plays a crucial role in minimally invasive procedures for effective disease management.</p></p>
