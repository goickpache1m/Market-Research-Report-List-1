<p><p>The electrical conduits market is primarily divided into two main types: flexible and rigid electrical conduits. Flexible electrical conduits are made from materials that allow bending and twisting, providing versatility for installations in tight or complex spaces. They offer resilience against vibrations and are ideal for dynamic environments. Rigid electrical conduits, on the other hand, are constructed from sturdy materials like metal or PVC, providing strong protection for electrical wiring in static applications, ensuring durability and safety in various settings.</p></p>
