<p><ul><li>Rotary Vane</li><li>Diaphragm</li><li>Liquid Ring</li><li>Venturi</li><li>Piston</li><li>Turbomolecular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial vacuum units market encompasses various types, each serving specific applications. Rotary vane units provide reliable, consistent suction for general vacuum needs. Diaphragm pumps are ideal for handling aggressive chemicals, offering chemical resistance. Liquid ring pumps excel in wet environments, maintaining efficiency by using liquid for sealing. Venturi systems leverage compressed air to create vacuum, suitable for lightweight tasks. Piston pumps provide high vacuum levels for demanding applications, while turbomolecular pumps enable extreme vacuum environments, often used in research and semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
