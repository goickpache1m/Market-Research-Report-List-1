<p><p>The Bulk Handling System Market features two primary types: Forklift Loading (FL) and Dedicated Hoist Load (DH). The FL type utilizes forklifts to move materials efficiently, offering flexibility in loading and unloading operations, making it suitable for diverse environments. In contrast, the DH type employs specialized hoists designed for heavy lifting, providing enhanced safety and efficiency for handling substantial loads. Both types cater to different operational needs, optimizing the handling of bulk materials across various industries.</p></p>
