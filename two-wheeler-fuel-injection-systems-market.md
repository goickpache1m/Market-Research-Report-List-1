<p><strong>The Two Wheeler Fuel Injection Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Fuel Injection System</li><li>Port Fuel Injection System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The two-wheeler fuel injection systems market comprises two main types: Direct Fuel Injection (DFI) and Port Fuel Injection (PFI). </p><p>DFI delivers fuel directly into the combustion chamber, enhancing efficiency, power output, and emissions control, making it suitable for high-performance bikes. Conversely, PFI injects fuel into the intake manifold, improving throttle response and fuel atomization, ideal for commuter bikes. Both systems aim to optimize overall engine performance, fuel efficiency, and reduce emissions, catering to varying consumer preferences and regulatory standards in the two-wheeler market.</p></p>
