<p><p>The semiconductor third-party testing services market encompasses various testing types essential for ensuring chip quality and performance. Laboratory testing involves evaluating raw materials and semiconductor components for reliability and compliance with specifications. Wafer testing assesses the functionality of individual chips on a silicon wafer, identifying defects early in the manufacturing process. Final testing is conducted on packaged chips to verify their performance under real-world conditions, ensuring they meet industry standards before reaching customers. Together, these services enhance product quality and reduce market risks.</p></p>
