<p><p>The medical power supply market includes various types, primarily categorized into AC/DC power supplies and DC/DC power supplies. AC/DC power supplies convert alternating current from outlets into direct current, suitable for powering medical devices like imaging equipment and patient monitors. In contrast, DC/DC power supplies convert one DC voltage level to another, often used in portable medical devices or within equipment to ensure stable operation and efficiency. Both types are vital for supporting reliable healthcare applications.</p></p>
