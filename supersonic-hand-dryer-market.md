<p><p>The supersonic hand dryer market is primarily divided into two types: hot air hand dryers and jet air hand dryers. Hot air hand dryers use warm air to evaporate moisture from hands, making them suitable for environments with lower humidity. In contrast, jet air hand dryers use high-velocity air jets to quickly blow water off hands, providing faster drying times. Both types are energy-efficient and promote hygiene, with varied applications in public restrooms, offices, and healthcare facilities.</p></p>
