<p><p>Transcatheter Aortic Valve Replacement (TAVR) service market primarily consists of two major types: Aortic Stenosis Replacement and Aortic Regurgitation Replacement. Aortic Stenosis Replacement targets patients with narrowed aortic valves, improving blood flow and relieving symptoms. Aortic Regurgitation Replacement addresses the condition where the aortic valve fails to close properly, allowing blood to leak back into the heart. Both procedures offer less invasive options for patients who may not be suitable for traditional surgical approaches, enhancing recovery times and overall patient outcomes.</p></p>
