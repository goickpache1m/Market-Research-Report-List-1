<p><p>The Pathological Diagnosis market encompasses various types, each essential for accurate disease identification. Histopathology focuses on the microscopic examination of tissue samples to detect abnormalities. Cell Pathology analyzes individual cells, aiding in the diagnosis of cancer and other diseases. Immunohistochemical Pathology employs antibodies to identify specific antigens in tissues, enhancing diagnostic accuracy. Molecular Pathology examines the genetic and molecular aspects of diseases, facilitating targeted therapies and personalized medicine. Together, these areas drive advancements in diagnostic precision and patient care.</p></p>
