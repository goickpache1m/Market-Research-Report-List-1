<p><p>The mobile payment technology market encompasses several types of transactions. Proximity payments involve tapping devices near payment terminals, while Near Field Communication (NFC) enables secure data exchange for payments. QR code payments allow users to scan codes for transactions. Remote payments facilitate online purchases via apps. SMS-based payments use text messages for transactions, and USSD/STK leverages mobile networks for secure payments. Direct operator billing integrates charges with user accounts, while digital wallets store payment information for easy access and transactions.</p></p>
