<p><p>A PSA (Pressure Swing Adsorption) gas generator is a system designed to produce high-purity gases, such as nitrogen and oxygen, by utilizing the adsorption process. This technology relies on the ability of certain materials to selectively adsorb specific gases under pressure, allowing for the separation and concentration of desired gases from a mixture. PSA gas generators are widely used across various industries, including pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and electronics, due to their efficiency and cost-effectiveness.</p><p>The PSA gas generator market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.2% during the forecast period, driven by increasing industrial demand for high-purity gases and the growing emphasis on sustainability. The integration of IoT and automation in PSA systems is also gaining traction, enhancing operational efficiency and monitoring capabilities.
