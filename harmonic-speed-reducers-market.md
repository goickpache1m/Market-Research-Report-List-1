<p><p>Harmonic speed reducers are essential components in precision motion control systems, categorized into three main types: Cup Style, Hat Style, and Pancake Style. Cup Style reducers feature a deep cup design for compactness and high torque transmission, making them ideal for limited-space applications. Hat Style reducers provide additional output torque with a broader profile, suitable for varied load requirements. Pancake Style reducers are ultra-thin, focusing on low-profile designs and high-speed applications, balancing efficiency and space constraints effectively.</p></p>
