<p><p>The warehouse freight elevator market primarily features two types: geared traction and gearless traction. </p><p>Geared traction elevators use a system of gears and pulleys, providing efficient lifting capabilities and precise control, making them suitable for heavy loads in various warehouse applications. </p><p>Gearless traction elevators, on the other hand, employ a direct-drive motor system, offering smoother operation and reduced energy consumption. These elevators are ideal for high-rise warehouses where efficiency and speed are critical for logistics and material handling.</p></p>
