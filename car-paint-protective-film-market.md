<p><p>The Car Paint Protective Film market primarily features two types: Thermoplastic Polyurethane (TPU) and Polyethylene (PE). TPU films are known for their superior flexibility, durability, and self-healing properties, making them ideal for protecting vehicle surfaces from scratches and environmental damage. Both types cater to different consumer needs, balancing cost and performance in the automotive protective film sector.</p></p>
