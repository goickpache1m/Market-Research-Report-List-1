<p><p>Crane markets are categorized into three main types based on design and functionality. Mobile cranes are versatile, easily transportable, and ideal for diverse job sites, allowing for quick setup and maneuverability. Tower cranes, often employed in high-rise construction, provide exceptional height and lifting capacity, anchored securely on-site. Mast cranes are similar to tower cranes but typically have a more rigid structure, offering stability and strength in heavy construction tasks. Each type serves specific applications within the construction industry.</p></p>
