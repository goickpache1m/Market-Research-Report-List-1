<p><ul><li>Mechanical Sweepers</li><li>Vacuum Air Sweepers</li><li>Regenerative Air Sweepers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The parking lot sweepers market features three main types: mechanical sweepers, vacuum air sweepers, and regenerative air sweepers. Mechanical sweepers use rotating brushes to lift debris, making them effective for larger areas. Vacuum air sweepers utilize suction combined with rotating brushes to capture fine dust and debris, ideal for detailed cleaning. Regenerative air sweepers combine both methods, using high-velocity air to dislodge dirt, improving efficiency and dust control. Each type caters to distinct cleaning requirements and surface conditions.</p></p>
