<p><p>The passenger car transmission fluids market is primarily divided into two types: manual transmission fluids and automatic transmission fluids. Manual transmission fluids are specifically formulated for vehicles with manual gearboxes, providing lubrication, cooling, and protection from wear. In contrast, automatic transmission fluids are designed for automatic transmissions, offering hydraulic properties and friction performance necessary for smooth shifting and operation. Both types are essential for maintaining vehicle efficiency, performance, and longevity, catering to the distinct requirements of different transmission systems.</p></p>
