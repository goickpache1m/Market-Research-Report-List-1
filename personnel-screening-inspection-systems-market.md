<p><p>The Personnel Screening Inspection Systems market includes advanced imaging technologies that enhance security by detecting concealed threats. X-Ray Backscatter Imaging Technology uses low-dose X-rays to reveal hidden items on a person's body, while Millimeter Wave Imaging Technology employs electromagnetic waves to create detailed images through clothing, spotting both metallic and non-metallic threats. Terahertz Imaging Technology operates in the terahertz frequency range, providing high-resolution images and effectively identifying various materials, thus bolstering safety in transportation and high-security venues.</p></p>
