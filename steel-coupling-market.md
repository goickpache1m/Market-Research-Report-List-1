<p><strong>The Steel Coupling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rigid Coupling</li><li>Flexible Coupling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel coupling market comprises two primary types: rigid coupling and flexible coupling. Rigid couplings are designed to connect two shafts tightly, maintaining alignment and ensuring efficient torque transmission without any shaft misalignment. In contrast, flexible couplings accommodate misalignments and provide dampening to vibrations, making them suitable for applications where slight shifts may occur. Both types are essential in various industries, including manufacturing and aerospace, catering to specific operational requirements while enhancing machinery performance and longevity.</p></p>
