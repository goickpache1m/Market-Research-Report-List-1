<p><strong>The Hydraulic Servo Actuators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Ended Servo Hydraulic Actuators</li><li>Double-Ended Servo Hydraulic Actuators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic servo actuator market comprises two primary types: single-ended and double-ended servo hydraulic actuators. Single-ended actuators utilize hydraulic pressure on one side of the piston, enabling precise control for applications needing efficient force in one direction. In contrast, double-ended actuators apply pressure on both sides, allowing for equal force in two directions, making them suitable for more dynamic applications. Both types are vital in industries like aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing for enhancing automation and performance.</p></p>
