<p><p>Automated Warehouse Systems encompass various types designed for efficient inventory management. Tray warehouses focus on storing and managing items within trays, enhancing organization and accessibility, particularly for smaller products. Pallet warehouses are designed for larger loads, utilizing pallets to store goods vertically, optimizing space and facilitating the handling of bulk items, making them ideal for high-capacity operations.</p></p>
