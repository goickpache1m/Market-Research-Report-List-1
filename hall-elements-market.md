<p><p>The Hall sensor market is segmented into two primary types based on output: analog and digital. Analog output Hall sensors provide a continuous voltage signal proportional to the magnetic field strength, making them suitable for precise measurements in applications like motor control and position sensing. In contrast, digital output Hall sensors deliver a binary signal, indicating the presence or absence of a magnetic field, which is ideal for applications requiring simple switching such as door position detection and speed monitoring.</p></p>
