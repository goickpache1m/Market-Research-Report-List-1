<p><p>The Gamma Knife System market comprises two primary types: Head Gamma Knife Systems and Body Gamma Knife Systems. Head Gamma Knife Systems are specialized for neurosurgical treatments, targeting brain tumors and vascular malformations with high precision. In contrast, Body Gamma Knife Systems, also known as stereotactic body radiotherapy, focus on non-cranial targets, treating tumors in various body parts like the lungs and liver. Both systems leverage advanced radiation technology to deliver effective and targeted therapies, enhancing patient outcomes significantly.</p></p>
