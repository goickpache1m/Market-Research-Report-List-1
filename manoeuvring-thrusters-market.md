<p><strong>The Manoeuvring Thrusters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tunnel Thrusters</li><li>Waterjet Bow Thrusters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manoeuvring thrusters market consists of various types, primarily tunnel thrusters and waterjet bow thrusters. Tunnel thrusters are cylindrical units installed in the hull, providing lateral thrust to enhance maneuverability, especially in tight spaces. Waterjet bow thrusters utilize high-pressure jets of water to generate thrust, offering precise control and higher efficiency, particularly in shallow waters. Both types are essential for improving vessel handling, stability, and docking performance, catering to different operational needs and vessel designs.</p></p>
