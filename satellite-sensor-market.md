<p><strong>The Satellite Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Satellite Sensor</li><li>Passive Satellite Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The satellite sensor market is divided into two main types: active and passive sensors. Active satellite sensors emit their own energy, such as radar or laser signals, to illuminate targets and capture reflected data, making them effective for all weather conditions and day/night operations. Conversely, passive satellite sensors detect natural sunlight or thermal energy emitted from objects, relying on available light sources. Each type serves distinct applications in earth observation, environmental monitoring, and disaster management, facilitating data collection for diverse industries.</p></p>
