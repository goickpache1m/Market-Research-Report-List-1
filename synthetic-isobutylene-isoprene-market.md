<p><p>The synthetic isobutylene isoprene rubber (IIR) market is primarily categorized into Regular IIR and Halogenated IIR. Regular IIR is known for its excellent elastomeric properties and resistance to aging and oxidation, making it suitable for tires and sealing applications. Halogenated IIR, modified through the addition of halogens, offers enhanced properties such as improved heat resistance and bonding capabilities, making it ideal for applications requiring enhanced durability and chemical resistance, such as automotive and industrial sectors.</p></p>
