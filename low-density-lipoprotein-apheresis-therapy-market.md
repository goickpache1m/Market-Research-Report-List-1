<p><p>Low-density Lipoprotein (LDL) Apheresis Therapy is a specialized treatment for patients with hypercholesterolemia, primarily targeting LDL cholesterol. The Double Filtration LDL Apheresis Therapy utilizes a two-step process to selectively filter and remove LDL particles from the bloodstream, enhancing efficiency and compatibility. On the other hand, Cascade Filtration LDL Apheresis Therapy employs a series of filtration steps that allow for the removal of LDL while retaining essential plasma proteins. Both methods aim to reduce cardiovascular risk by lowering harmful cholesterol levels effectively.</p></p>
