<p><p>The whey protein ingredient market consists of three main types: Whey Protein Concentrate (WPC), Whey Protein Isolate (WPI), and Hydrolyzed Whey Protein. WPC contains around 70-80% protein, retaining beneficial compounds and fats, making it cost-effective for various applications. WPI boasts a higher protein content of 90% or more and is low in lactose, ideal for those seeking pure protein. Hydrolyzed Whey Protein is pre-digested for faster absorption, often used in sports nutrition and medical supplements for quick recovery and improved digestion.</p></p>
